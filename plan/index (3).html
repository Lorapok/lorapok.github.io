      // why we exist
      
Software should be a public good

      
The best tools belong to everyone — not locked behind subscriptions or controlled by investors.

    

    

      
🔓
Radically open
Every line is public. Fork it, audit it, ship it. No premium tiers, no dark patterns.

      
🌍
Built for everyone
We design for developers in Dhaka as much as in Berlin. Bandwidth-aware, dependency-light.

      
🤝
Community-owned
No single company controls the roadmap. Decisions in the open — PRs, issues, discussions.

      
✦
AI-augmented
We use AI to lower the barrier — docs, onboarding, tooling, and blog posts, all AI-assisted.
